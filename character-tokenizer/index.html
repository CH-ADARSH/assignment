What is Character Tokenization?

Character tokenization is the process of breaking down a text into its smallest individual units: characters. Unlike word or subword tokenization, it treats each letter, number, symbol, and even spaces as distinct tokens.

This method is simple and ensures that every input can be represented, making it robust against out-of-vocabulary words. However, it results in longer sequences of tokens and doesn't capture semantic meaning as effectively as higher-level tokenization.
